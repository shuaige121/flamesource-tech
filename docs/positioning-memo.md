# Flame Source Technology — Positioning Memo

## Company Identity
**Flame Source = Singapore-based applied automation company**

We design and deploy workflow automation systems for operations teams.
We are NOT a SaaS product company. We are a solutions delivery company.

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
- Singapore SMEs with 20-200 staff
- Operations-heavy teams drowning in manual processes
- Sectors: F&B, Recruitment agencies, Financial services back-office, Education/consulting sales teams
- Decision maker: Ops director, business owner, CFO

## One-Line Positioning
> We automate the manual workflows your team shouldn't still be doing — hiring, payroll, frontline communication, sales enablement.

## Solution Pillars (in order of priority)
1. **Recruitment Operations** — Automated screening, scoring, ranking
2. **Finance & Back-Office** — Payroll, reconciliation, invoicing automation
3. **Frontline Multilingual Execution** — AR smart glasses + voice AI for restaurant/F&B staff
4. **Sales Enablement** — Real-time AI coaching + training for sales teams
5. **Creative Production** — Music arrangement acceleration (lower priority)

## Primary CTA
"Talk to Us" / "Book an Intro"

## Flame Source OS Role
- Internal delivery platform, NOT the homepage hero
- Appears as: "Powered by Flame Source OS"
- Gets its own secondary page, not the main narrative
- Technical depth lives on /solutions or a dedicated tech page

## Copy Rules
- Lead with business outcomes, not AI capabilities
- Structure: Problem → Workflow → What changed → Proof
- "AI" appears where needed, never as the subject
- Every metric must have evidence or gets deleted
- No placeholders, no empty shells

## Homepage Narrative (5-second test)
A first-time visitor should immediately understand:
1. What we do (automate operations workflows)
2. Who we serve (Singapore SMEs / ops teams)
3. How to start (Talk to Us)
