01 Recruitment Operations

Automated Screening, Scoring, Ranking

The problem: Your consultants start every morning buried in resumes instead of talking to candidates. With the rise of polished, template-driven applications, quick visual scanning is no longer reliable — strong candidates get buried while surface-level ones float to the top.

What We Deliver

We deploy an automated screening and ranking system that reads every incoming resume, scores candidates against your role-specific criteria, and surfaces a ranked shortlist each morning. The system handles formatting inconsistencies, parses real experience from filler, and flags candidates whose backgrounds genuinely match — regardless of how well the resume is written. Your consultants start the day with a shortlist, not a stack.

Delivery

Deployed in approximately one month. Most of that time is iterative alignment — understanding how your team evaluates candidates, which criteria matter for different role types, and calibrating the ranking to match your judgment. When you flag a misranked candidate, we adjust and redeploy within hours.

Best Fit

Recruitment consultancies and in-house hiring teams in Singapore processing high volumes of applications across multiple roles.

Talk to Us

What Changed

  • Screening time reduced from hours to minutes
  • Consultants spend more of their day in conversations, not reading documents
  • Adjustments deployed same-day when rankings need recalibrating
02 Finance & Back-Office

Payroll, Reconciliation, Invoicing Automation

The problem: Your operations staff handle both tenancy management and finance workflows. Operational urgencies during the month push finance tasks to the back of the queue, leaving invoicing and reconciliation backlogged at month-end. Manual data entry across spreadsheets creates errors that compound downstream.

What We Deliver

We deliver two connected automation solutions. The first structures your operations workflow — tenant records, unit status tracking, lease timelines, and task scheduling — into a single managed system. The second automates the finance workflow — invoice generation from operational data, payment matching, and reconciliation reporting. Because both solutions share the same data, an updated lease automatically flows into the correct invoice schedule.

Delivery

Delivered within approximately one month. The iterative process surfaces edge cases — partial-month billing, multi-unit accounts, variable schedules — through back-and-forth testing. When you identify a scenario we have not accounted for, the fix is typically delivered within a day.

Best Fit

Property services companies, facilities management firms, and operations-heavy SMEs in Singapore where the same staff juggle both operational and financial workflows.

Talk to Us

What Changed

  • Monthly close process significantly shortened
  • Manual data-entry errors eliminated — invoices match tenancy records automatically
  • Staff freed from end-of-month finance tasks to focus on tenant relationships
03 Frontline Multilingual Execution

AR Smart Glasses + Voice AI for F&B Staff

The problem: Your service staff learn dish names and basic flows quickly. The persistent problem is nuanced customer requests — "less oil", "crispier", "no chili" — the modifiers where dining preferences actually live. These get lost in translation, causing wrong orders, food waste, and frustration for staff who know they misunderstood but cannot pinpoint what was said.

What We Deliver

We deploy AR smart glasses that provide real-time subtitles during customer interactions — including the specific modifiers — displayed in the staff member's field of view. The interaction stays natural; no need to ask customers to repeat themselves. Separately, we build a web-based training app that staff use during breaks to practise real customer phrases with spaced repetition. Over time, staff internalise the language and rely on the glasses less. Our hardware team handles procurement and supply chain to keep costs manageable.

Delivery

Deployed in approximately one month. The iterative process is especially important here because modifier vocabulary is domain-specific — "dry" means something different for noodles than for vegetables. We work closely with your kitchen and floor managers to build an accurate phrase library, updating translations same-day when staff flag anything unnatural.

Best Fit

F&B operators in Singapore with multilingual frontline staff — especially restaurants where nuanced customer requests directly affect service quality.

Talk to Us

What Changed

  • Wrong orders from modifier misunderstandings effectively eliminated
  • Staff visibly more confident during service — customers no longer need to repeat or simplify requests
  • Several staff members progressed to understanding most modifiers without checking the glasses
04 Sales Enablement

Real-Time AI Coaching for Sales Teams

The problem: In customer-facing businesses, the quality of the conversation determines whether a sale closes. New staff know your products but lack the instinct for when to recommend, how to handle objections, and how to close naturally. Experienced staff perform well but have no scalable way to transfer what they know. Traditional training — shadowing, role-play, manuals — helps, but there is always a gap between training and live performance.

What We Deliver

We deploy a real-time coaching system that transcribes live customer conversations and provides staff with contextual prompts — suggested responses, product recommendations, and objection-handling cues — in real time. The coaching is subtle and non-intrusive. For new hires, we also build a practice partner: a conversational training tool that simulates realistic customer interactions and adapts to each person's weak spots, so they can rehearse before going on the floor.

Delivery

Each deployment takes approximately one month. The majority of time is spent understanding your sales process, common customer objections, and what "good" looks like in your specific context. A creative studio has very different dynamics from a hair salon, so the coaching logic is tailored for each business.

Best Fit

Customer-facing sales teams in Singapore — creative studios, consultancies, salons, and service businesses where the conversation is the product.

Talk to Us

What Changed

  • New staff productive on the floor significantly sooner — handling customer conversations with confidence from week one
  • Experienced staff report the system occasionally surfaces recommendations they had not considered
  • The practice partner gives nervous new hires a low-pressure environment to build skills before going live
05 Creative Production

Music Arrangement Acceleration

The problem: Your creative ideas come together fast — melodies and song structures take shape quickly. But the arrangement and instrumentation phase is a bottleneck. Layering instruments, creating production-ready arrangements, and iterating on sonic direction takes weeks per track. For an artist building momentum, that delay means fewer releases, slower audience growth, and lost creative energy.

What We Deliver

We deploy tools that accelerate the arrangement and instrumentation workflow. The system takes your core musical ideas — melody, chord progression, song structure — and generates arrangement drafts with instrumentation options. You review, select, and refine from these drafts rather than building every layer from scratch. Creative decisions stay entirely with you; the repetitive production work moves dramatically faster. The output integrates with standard music production software.

Delivery

Delivered in approximately one month. The iterative process is essential because musical taste is subjective and personal. Through fast back-and-forth, we refine the system's understanding of your sonic preferences until the drafts consistently land close to your intended direction.

Best Fit

Independent musicians and emerging artists in Singapore whose creative output outpaces their production capacity.

Talk to Us

What Changed

  • Arrangement turnaround compressed from weeks to days per track
  • Creative momentum maintained — full versions heard while the idea is still fresh
  • Final output quality validated by the artist's award-winning mentors
