Case Study

Real Problem. Real Outcome.

Recruitment · AI Screening

AI pipeline replaced 80% of recruiter workload at SGD 0.01 per resume

Client

A Singapore recruitment consultancy whose consultants spent most of their week screening resumes instead of talking to candidates

Challenge

At ~SGD 30/hr, a mid-level recruiter was spending over SGD 960 per week on sourcing and screening — work that never touches a candidate. The economics of manual screening simply don’t scale.

Outcome

An AI pipeline built on Brave Search API and Claude now handles sourcing and screening at ~SGD 0.01 per resume. 80% of recruiter time freed up for candidate conversations — the work that actually closes placements.
