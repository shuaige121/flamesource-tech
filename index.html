Solutions

What We Build

Real products, real deployments. Here's what our team delivers.

AI Agent & Automation

Multi-agent orchestration systems that run autonomously. We've built AI-driven project management bots (Claude AI + Notion + voice), LLM tool-gating frameworks, and full autonomous agent platforms where AI handles judgment calls while machines handle deterministic tasks.

Platform & API Engineering

Edge-deployed APIs and integration platforms on Cloudflare Workers. From marketing APIs handling OAuth and 50+ endpoints, to multi-project gateway control planes and cross-platform messaging toolkits connecting Telegram, Feishu, and WhatsApp.

IoT & Hardware-Software

Bridging physical devices and cloud intelligence. Our founding team holds 200+ patents in wireless communication and precision hardware — we design IoT architectures from device firmware to cloud analytics, proven at scale in 150+ countries.

AI Creative Tools

Production-grade AI creative software — a CapCut-style video editor with CLI and AI control powered by Remotion and GPU acceleration, and an AI arrangement engine that turns melodies into full multi-track compositions with drums, bass, piano, and chords.
