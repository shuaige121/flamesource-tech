What We Do

Software Between Sensors
and Decisions

We build software that sits between existing sensor infrastructure and operational decisions. Our platform turns raw monitoring data into actionable intelligence — without replacing core hardware or disrupting existing workflows.

Core Modules

Four Products, One Platform

Each module operates independently or as part of an integrated analytics stack.

Water Quality Anomaly Detection

Detect unusual patterns in multi-parameter sensor streams in near real time, enabling faster response to potential water quality incidents. Supports pH, dissolved oxygen, turbidity, conductivity, and temperature correlation analysis.

Predictive Risk Analytics

Forecast environmental risks — such as algal bloom events in reservoirs — days before they become visible, enabling preemptive operational adjustments. Models trained on multi-year sensor and weather data achieve high predictive accuracy based on published research benchmarks.

Sensor Health Management

Identify sensor drift, faults, missing data, and connectivity issues automatically, before bad data affects downstream decisions. Designed for large-scale IoT deployments in tropical outdoor environments.

Automated Reporting

Generate formatted monitoring reports in seconds — complete with trend charts, anomaly highlights, and AI-written executive summaries in English and Chinese. Replaces hours of manual data extraction and report writing.
